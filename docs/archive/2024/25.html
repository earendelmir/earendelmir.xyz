I've been busy.
This is my excuse. It's a valid one, though, I swear!

My life changed so much in these last 4 months that I don't even know what to say first.
Here's a preview of what I will write about in the next few posts:

  • I have a job
  • I moved to a new city
  • I have a girlfriend
  • I have a car
  • I have lost weight
  • I have made new friends at work

I have so much now! It's almost overwhelming how well things have been going for me lately.

This is a quick post, just to say that this site is not dead.
I miss it terribly! I miss writing for it, for those few readers I have. I hope some of you will see this and think "Oh, good! I missed that guy"... well, that guy is back! Get ready!
